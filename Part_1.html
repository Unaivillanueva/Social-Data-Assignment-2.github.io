Question: Why you think I want the axes to be the same for both years?

If the axes changed with the data, it would be difficult to compare how the values evolved during the years. Since we maintain the same axes for both, it is really easy to notice how the cases of prostitution and vehicle theft have decreased from 2003 to 2015.
